      <h3 style="color: #483D8B;">Know Your Engagement Ratio</h3>  
      <p>Organizations that sustain a ratio of 8 enthusiasts to every 1 detractor — be they employees or customers — are reaping great benefits.</p>
      <p>e.mote<sup style="font-size: 60%">®</sup> provides an at-a-glance indication of how close you are to this golden ratio by displaying each level of engagement in comparison to the others. </p>
        <p>In addition, the e.mote<sup style="font-size: 60%">®</sup> scorecard displays a spectrum of the emotions people are expressing and how often. </p>
        <p>Finally, the e.mote<sup style="font-size: 60%">®</sup> scorecard gives you the “why” behind each emotion and engagement level in the Comments. Like a compass, the verbatim collected from your respondents helps you continually improve your engagement ratio.</p></div>
        <h4 style="color: #483d8b;">Dig a little deeper…</h4>
